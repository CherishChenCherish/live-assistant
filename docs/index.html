Features

🎙

Real-time Transcription

Powered by Whisper. Captures audio from your mic, Zoom, Teams, or any meeting app. Auto-detects which app you're using.

💬

AI Response Generation

Detects real interview questions and generates natural spoken English answers tailored to your background.

💻

Technical Interview Support

For coding questions: generates working code + verbal explanation + complexity analysis. Python, SQL, system design.

📎

Context Upload

Upload your resume, job description, and interviewer info. The AI uses this to craft personalized, relevant answers.

🔮

Follow-up Predictions

Predicts the interviewer's likely next question so you can prepare ahead.

🆘

SOS Button

Stuck? Press S for a natural stalling phrase to buy yourself thinking time.

📷

Screenshot OCR

Ctrl+V a screenshot of the coding problem. OCR extracts the text and generates a solution.

⏱

Pace Monitor

Tracks your speaking speed (WPM) and answer length. Stay in the sweet spot — not too fast, not too slow.

How It Works

1

Download & Launch

Download the app, run it. Opens in your browser at localhost. No account needed.

2

Upload & Configure

Upload your resume and JD. Select your audio device. The app auto-detects your meeting software.

3

Interview with Confidence

See real-time transcription and AI-generated answers. Use keyboard shortcuts for seamless control.
